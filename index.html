About CK's

CK's Window Installation & Doors is led by Chris Kent, a local professional who believes good work should be simple to understand and easy to trust. We start with a quick conversation about your goals, then recommend products that fit your home, budget, and timeline. Our installers protect floors and furnishings, keep noise low, and leave your space clean. We check fitment, shimming, flashing, and sealants with the same care we would use in our own homes. Whether you need a single replacement unit or a full project, you will get the same attention, honest guidance, and dependable follow-up.

What you can expect

Every project follows a clear plan. We confirm measurements, schedule a convenient start date, and lay out the steps so there are no surprises. During installation we verify level and plumb, insulate gaps, and seal against wind and water. We test operation, adjust hardware, and review maintenance so your new windows or doors perform as promised. Afterward we walk the site with you, answer questions, and provide documentation for warranties where applicable. You can count on respectful crews, steady communication, and results that look good and last season after season.

Services

We handle replacements, new construction installs, and specialty work for homes across Idaho Falls and nearby towns. If your goal is comfort, lower energy use, or a fresh look, we can recommend Alpine, AMSCO, Milgard, Sierra Pacific, and Basement Window Systems that balance value and performance. We also cut and finish basement egress openings, install strong and smooth patio or entry doors, and complete trim with clean lines. Glass repair, screen repair, and weatherization round out our service list, giving you one reliable team for most door and window needs year round.

Windows

Great windows start with the right match for your space, climate, and budget. We install and replace units from Alpine, AMSCO, Milgard, Sierra Pacific, and Basement Window Systems, explaining frame materials and glass packages so you can choose with confidence. For new construction, we coordinate with your builder to keep schedules tight and details crisp. If you need an egress window, we handle the cut-out, support, finish, and interior trim so everything looks intentional. Our installs focus on fit, insulation, and weather sealing, which helps reduce drafts, noise, and utility costs for the long run.

Doors

Entry and patio doors should feel solid, close smoothly, and seal tight in every season. We install replacement doors and new construction doors with careful attention to thresholds, flashing, and lock alignment. Milgard options are available for a durable and polished finish. If you are considering storm doors, we can recommend styles that protect your primary door and add light without sacrificing privacy. Our approach puts precision first, from squaring the frame to setting hardware, so day-to-day use is easy. Expect a clean look, dependable performance, and clear guidance on care and maintenance.

Trim and More

Good trim makes a big difference. We install paint grade, stained grade, and flex mouldings that frame your windows and doors with clean lines and tight joints. For existing homes, we match profiles and finishes so upgrades blend with your current style. We also take care of glass and screen repair to restore function without replacing full units. Weatherization services help seal problem areas, improve comfort, and protect against moisture. Each task is planned to limit disruption and keep your home tidy. You get careful work, fair pricing, and results that are easy to live with.
